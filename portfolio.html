The Jobify Platform

MAO DAO

A fully decentralized community ecosystem built around the $MAO token, giving holders real governance power, real rewards, and a real stake in how the project grows.

Built on realms.today, MAO DAO gave every token holder a real voice. Members stake $MAO, submit proposals, and vote on the decisions that shape the ecosystem, genuine decentralized governance, not just the aesthetic of it.
